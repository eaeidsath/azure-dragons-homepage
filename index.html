About Us

We are a Final Fantasy XIV Online Free Company established in 2017. Ran by our president Pia Celeste and vice president Jaffa Bong, we are a close-knit community of MMO enthusiasts whose interests span all aspects of the game. Through the FC, we host events such as map nights, learning parties for Extreme and Savage, and fun competitions such as glamour contests and treasure hunts. If you are interested in joining our FC, we would love to have you! Please check the Contact Us section below to get in touch and become a member of the Azure Dragons!
